Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

We guarantee a 30-minute response time for all emergency calls within the New Haven area. Our teams are on standby 24/7, ready to deploy with all necessary equipment.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes, we work directly with all major insurance providers and can help manage your claim from start to finish. We provide detailed documentation and work directly with adjusters to streamline the process.

What types of water damage do you handle?

We handle all categories of water damage including clean water, gray water, and black water emergencies. This includes flooding, burst pipes, sewage backups, storm damage, and more.

How long does the restoration process take?

The timeline varies depending on the extent of damage, but most residential projects are completed within 3-5 days. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial assessment.

What steps do you take to prevent mold growth?

We use industrial dehumidifiers and specialized drying equipment, along with antimicrobial treatments when necessary. Our process ensures moisture levels are brought to safe levels to prevent mold growth.
