Understanding Water Damage Categories & Classes: A Complete Guide

January 30, 2025

Water damage comes in three distinct categories: clean water from sanitary sources, gray water containing significant contamination, and black water with dangerous pathogens. Understanding these categories and their four classes, based on evaporation rates, is crucial for proper restoration. Professional restoration follows a systematic approach: emergency assessment, water extraction, drying, cleaning, and repairs. Prevention includes regular maintenance of roofs, gutters, and appliances, while installing water detection devices. Property owners should immediately contact professionals when damage affects multiple rooms, involves structural concerns, or shows signs of mold growth. Quick response is essential for effective restoration and minimizing damage.
